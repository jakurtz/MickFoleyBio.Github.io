Notable Moments of Micks Life:
  • June 7th 1965 - Born in Bloomington, Indiana.
  • 1983 - Starts his training at Dominic DeNucci's wrestling school.
  • September 5th 1991 - Debuts as 'Cactus Jack' on WCW televison.
  • April 6th 1993 - Wrestled noted rival 'Vader' for the first time.
  • 1994 - Left WCW and began traveling various promotions (including ECW).
  • 1995 - Helped pioneer the "hardcore" Wrestling scene in Japan that he's known for today.
  • April 1st 1996 - Debuts on WWF's Monday Night Raw.
  • December 29th 1998 - Wins the WWF Championship live on Mondy Night Raw.
  • January 31st 1999 - Wins the WWF Championship for the second time duriing SuperBowl XXXVII's halftime show.
  • August 22nd 1999 - Wins the WWF Championship for the 3rd and final time at Summer-Slam 1999.
  • April 2nd 2000 - Retires from full time in-ring competiton.
  • March 1st 2004 - Returns to WWE Competition for various "semi-feuds".
  • September 18th 2008 - Debuts in TNA (also known as) Total Nonstop Action.
  • November 2nd 2011 - Returns to WWE.
  • August 2012 - Unable to be medically cleared to return to the ring.
  • April 6th 2013 - Inducted into the WWE Hall of Fame.
